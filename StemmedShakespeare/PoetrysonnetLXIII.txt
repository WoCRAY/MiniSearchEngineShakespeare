sonnet lxiii sonnet lxiii against my love shall be as i am now with time injuri hand crush'd and o'erworn when hour have drain'd his blood and fill'd his brow with line and wrinkl when his youth morn hath travell'd on to age steepi night and all those beauti whereof now he king are vanish or vanish'd out of sight steal away the treasur of his spring for such a time do i now fortifi against confound age cruel knife that he shall never cut from memori my sweet love beauti though my lover life his beauti shall in these black line be seen and they shall live and he in them still green
